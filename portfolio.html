Week 8 - Festival of Economics

During the festival, I attended the 'Reflections on Economics' talk with Andy Haldane and Diane Coyle. Andy argued that the private sector, rather than local or national government, were more useful in the regeneration of US cities, and that this was something the levelling up agenda could learn from. To support this argument I have made this chart that clearly shows a significantly higher level of employment through the private sector.

Week 8 - Festival of Economics

This chart also supports the argument. Although government investment seems to be overtaking the private sector, for the first three quarters it is clear that private investment is higher. Both charts support the argument by showing the private sector has provided higher employment and more investment in 2022.
